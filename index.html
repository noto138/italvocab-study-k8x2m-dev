Italian Vocab Practice - Instructions & Updates

How to Use:

Practice Tab: Select your difficulty level and choose which word categories you want to practice. You can include or exclude your custom words, or practice with custom words only. Click "Get New Words" to start practicing with randomly selected vocabulary.

Manage Words Tab: Search through all vocabulary to see if words already exist, import/export word lists, and add your own custom words. Custom words will appear in all difficulty levels when enabled.

Features:

  • 🔊 Click the speaker icon to hear Italian pronunciation
  • 📋 Click the clipboard icon to copy words to your clipboard
  • 📖 Click the book icon to look up words in an Italian dictionary
  • Con Click the conjugation button (for verbs) to see verb conjugations
  • 👁️ Use hint buttons to show/hide gender markers and English translations
  • ⭐ Add custom words that persist across all difficulty levels
  • 🔍 Live search through all vocabulary as you type

Recent Updates:

  • Added conjugation icon for verbs to view verb conjugations
  • Added custom words filtering options (exclude/include/only)
  • Improved search results to show each word only once with consolidated levels
  • Enhanced word management with better visual feedback
  • Added backup and restore functionality for custom words

Tips:

Start with beginner level and gradually work your way up. Use the custom words feature to add vocabulary that's specific to your interests or learning goals. The search function is great for checking if a word already exists before adding it as a custom word.
